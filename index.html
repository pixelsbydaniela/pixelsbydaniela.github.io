Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Pixels by Daniela & The Archive · Updated May 2026

How far in advance should I book? +
For weddings and events, 6–12 months ahead is strongly recommended — peak season (September–April) books out fast. Portrait sessions can usually be booked 2–4 weeks out.
Do you travel outside Cape Town? +
Yes — available throughout South Africa and internationally. Local Cape Town shoots (within 50km) carry no travel surcharge. Further destinations are quoted separately.
How long until I receive my photos? +
Real estate: 24–48 hours · Portraits: 7–10 business days · Weddings and events: 4–6 weeks. Every image is individually edited. If you have an urgent deadline, mention it when you enquire.
Do you edit every photo? +
Yes. Every delivered image is individually colour-graded, exposed, and retouched by hand. Quality always over quantity.
What if the weather is bad on shoot day? +
For outdoor portraits, we'll reschedule at no extra cost if needed. For weddings, we shoot regardless — rain can be genuinely beautiful.
How do I receive my photos? +
Via a private, password-protected online gallery. Download full-resolution files directly. The gallery stays live for 90 days.
What payment methods do you accept? +
EFT bank transfer only. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due the day before the shoot.
Who owns the photos after the shoot? +
Copyright remains with Pixels by Daniela. You receive a personal-use licence — print, share, and use freely for personal purposes. Commercial use requires a separate licence.

The Basics

How does The Archive work? +
Get in touch to lock in your date. Once confirmed, we ship a vintage camcorder kit to your nearest Pargo drop-off point before your event. You and your guests film in classic, nostalgic style. After the party, return the kit via Pargo using a digital barcode sent to your phone via SMS. Once it lands back with us, we extract your footage and craft your highlight reel if you chose that package.
What comes in the rental kit? +
Every Archive kit contains: 1 × Vintage Sony Handycam · 2 × High-capacity batteries (fully charged) · 1 × External battery charger · 1 × Memory card/tape · Instruction cards with QR code tutorial · Shot inspiration guide · Seeded thank you note · Return packaging and Pargo return label.

Delivery & Returns

When and how do I return the kit? +
Drop the kit at any Pargo point by 12:00 PM on the first business day after your event (usually Monday morning). It's entirely paperless — you'll receive a return barcode via SMS, just show it at the counter and hand over the box.
When will I receive the kit? +
We dispatch at least 3 business days before your event. You'll get an SMS from Pargo when it's ready to collect.

Power & Batteries

Will the battery die mid-party? +
Not on our watch. We include two high-capacity batteries and a fast charger. Just swap them over and you'll never miss a moment.

The Footage

How do I get my video files? +
Within 21 days of receiving the kit back, we email you a private Google Drive or WeTransfer link with your raw footage and edited highlight reel.
Can I use the footage on social media? +
Yes — full personal rights for private and social media use. We just ask that you credit @thearchive.cpt when sharing. A privacy option is available for an additional 15% fee.
What if the equipment isn't working when I receive it? +
Contact us within 12 hours of collection via WhatsApp or email at thearchive.cpt@gmail.com. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund.
