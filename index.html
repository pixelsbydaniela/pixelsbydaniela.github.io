Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Pixels by Daniela · Updated May 2026

How far in advance should I book? +
For weddings and events, 6–12 months ahead is strongly recommended — peak season (September–April) books out fast. Portrait sessions can usually be booked 2–4 weeks out. Reach out as soon as you have a date in mind.
Do you travel outside Cape Town? +
Yes — available throughout South Africa and internationally. Local Cape Town shoots (within 50km) carry no travel surcharge. Further destinations are quoted separately to cover fuel, accommodation where required, and time. International destination shoots — including Europe — are quoted in full.
How long until I receive my photos? +
Turnaround times: Real estate: 24–48 hours · Portraits: 7–10 business days · Weddings and events: 4–6 weeks. Every image is individually edited — nothing is batch-processed. If you have an urgent deadline, mention it when you enquire.
Do you edit every photo? +
Yes. Every delivered image is individually colour-graded, exposed, and retouched by hand. Nothing gets a one-click filter. You'll receive only the images that meet the standard — quality always over quantity.
What if the weather is bad on shoot day? +
For outdoor portraits, we'll monitor the forecast together and reschedule at no extra cost if needed. For weddings, we shoot regardless — rain can be genuinely beautiful, and I'll always find a way to make it work. Weather is not grounds for cancellation at weddings.
How do I receive my photos? +
Via a private, password-protected online gallery. You'll receive a link to download full-resolution files directly. The gallery stays live for 90 days — download everything before it expires. USB delivery available on request at an additional fee.
Do you offer custom packages? +
Yes. The Services page shows starting-from pricing, but every project is different. Get in touch with the details of your shoot and I'll put together a tailored quote.
What payment methods do you accept? +
EFT bank transfer only. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due the day before the shoot. Your date is not secured until the deposit is received and acknowledged in writing.
Who owns the photos after the shoot? +
Copyright remains with Pixels by Daniela, as is standard in professional photography. You receive a full personal-use licence — you can print, share, and use the images freely for personal purposes. Commercial use (paid advertising, product packaging, resale) requires a separate commercial licence. See the full Terms & Conditions for detail.
What about The Archive camcorder rentals? +
The Archive is our vintage camcorder rental service — launching soon! You'll need a valid SA ID or passport and a refundable security deposit. Full rental terms are on The Archive page. Register your interest now to be first to know when we go live.

Rental Agreement

The Archive by Pixels by Daniela · Effective on booking · Cape Town, South Africa

This agreement applies to all camcorder rentals through The Archive. By completing a booking you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to these terms.

1. Eligibility

  • You must be 18 years or older to rent from The Archive
  • Valid South African ID or passport required at time of collection
  • Business bookings require company registration details

2. Bookings & Payment

Full payment is required at the time of reservation. Rentals are calculated per calendar day. Minimum rental period is 1 day. Payment is by EFT only.

A refundable security deposit (disclosed at booking, typically R500–R2,000 depending on equipment) is required at collection and returned within 3 business days of a clean, undamaged return.

3. Collection & Return

  • Equipment is collected from and returned to Cape Town (address provided at booking confirmation)
  • Return is due by 10:00am on the final rental day
  • Late returns are charged at the full daily rate per additional day
  • Equipment must be returned in the same condition as collected

4. Care of Equipment

The renter accepts full responsibility for the equipment from collection to return. This includes:

  • Protecting equipment from water, impact, and extreme temperatures
  • Using only supplied accessories and cables
  • Not attempting repairs or modifications
  • Keeping equipment secure at all times

5. Damage & Loss

The renter is liable for the full cost of repair or replacement in the event of damage or loss. Costs are first deducted from the security deposit; any remainder is the renter's responsibility and must be settled within 14 days.

Normal wear is accepted. Accidental damage, water damage, and loss are not covered and remain the renter's liability. We strongly recommend personal insurance for high-value rentals.

6. Cancellations

  • More than 72 hours' notice: full refund minus R150 admin fee
  • Within 72 hours: 50% refund
  • Same-day cancellations: no refund

7. Permitted Use

Equipment is rented for personal and professional creative use only. Equipment may not be sub-rented or transferred to a third party. Commercial productions requiring insurance certificates should contact us in advance.
